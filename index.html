Help.me is a responsive web-app that promotes
focus and concentration.

According to researchers at Johns Hopkins, the best way to stay focused is through managing time. But who has time for that? Help.me is an interactive app in which you tell us what you're doing and we'll do all the busy work.

Homework

All social media blocked. 5 minute breaks every half-hour.

Meeting

All publishing, social media, video streaming blocked with 2 minute breaks every 30 minutes.

Casual

Nothing blocked. Casual reminders to keep you focused.

S.O.S. Homework

Website will go into defense mode. No breaks. No social media.

S.O.S. Meeting

Website will go into defense mode. All publishing, social media, video streaming blocked.

Productive

If you're spending too much time on one page, we'll remind you.

We'll update you in the coming weeks.
